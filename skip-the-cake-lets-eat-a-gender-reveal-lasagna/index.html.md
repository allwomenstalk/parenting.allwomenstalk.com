# Skip the Cake, Let's Eat a Gender Reveal Lasagna ...

Yes, you read that right. A lasagna that reveals the gender of your baby and will be the talk of your gender reveal party. Move over cake, lasagna is here.

Gender reveal parties have been around for quite some time and normally it is cake that has been dyed blue or pink and covered in white frosting. When you cut into it, the color inside the cake reveals the gender of the soon-to-be-born baby.

However, like with any trend, it can get old or tired and you begin to look for something new. And not everybody likes or can have cake. Yet with the lasagna, you can serve an alternative to the sweet treat and have something new that will be the talk of your gender reveal party, beside the gender of your baby.

If you’re looking to buy this special lasagna, you can get it only from the nationwide chain Villa Italian Kitchen. This surprise-inside-lasagna costs 139.99 US dollars, serves twelve, comes with garlic rolls, and your choice of garden, Caesar or Greek salad. Rather costly, but hey, it serves twelve.

Here’s how this works: your doctor will write down the sex of your baby and put it in an envelope. You DON’T open it and give it to the baker at the restaurant and they will give you a lasagna with the color of your baby’s gender inside. When you get home and cut it open, ta-da, a gender reveal lasagna.

As I stated, $140 for a lasagna is a bit much. So if you want to make it at home, all you need is a recipe for lasagna. Of course you’ll need noodles, alfredo or tomato sauce, ricotta and mozzarella. And to create the color, all you need is to add the food coloring to the cheese.
