# 7 Tips on How to Develop Social Skills in a Child ...

If you are looking for tips on how to develop social skills in a child, look no further because you’ve come to the right place. Developing social skills at a younger age is very important for building and maintaining friendships. Good social skills will help your child display good manners, have better social interactions, be more considerate of the feelings of others and communicate more efficiently with the ones around them. Making friends can be a challenge for some kids, but there are a lot of things you can do that will help them overcome this obstacle. Here are a few very helpful tips on how to develop social skills in a child:

## 1. Give Plenty of Encouragement

One of the most important tips on how to develop social skills in a child that I could tell you is to give them plenty of encouragement, not only when they manage to reach one of their goals but also when they fail. Be there by their side and show them understanding, love and support.

## 2. Invite Friends Home

Another very effective thing you can do if you want to help your kid develop better social skills and make more friends is to simply invite some of his colleagues or friends home. This way, your kid will be more relaxed if they are in their own home and they will be more willing to work on improving their social skills.

## 3. Teach Them the Basic Rules of Friendship

Teach your kids the basic rules of friendship. Encourage them to share their toys with other kids, use words when they are mad (make sure they don’t use bad words) and to be gentle when they touch others and pets. If they follow these very simple rules, they will surely make a lot of friends in no time.

## 4. Reward Positive Behaviors

Always reward your kid’s positive behaviors. Praise them when they demonstrate gentleness and sharing. You can also buy them a little gift, something not very expensive but that they really like (their favorite chocolate, you could watch a movie together or you could do any other activity they enjoy as a reward for their good behavior).

## 5. Organize Social Events for Kids

If you want to help your kids develop better social skills, one of the best things you can do is to organize social events for kids. Here I’m talking about hosting parties for kids in your home. Just invite your kid’s colleagues and also the kids of all your neighbors.

## 6. Know the Limits

Make sure you know your child’s limits and don’t make them socialize if they are tired of socializing and if all they want is to get a little rest. Try to be sensitive to their mood because this way, you will increase their interest in planning future play dates.

## 7. Teach Them to Appreciate the Ones around Them

Teach your kids to appreciate the ones around them and to acknowledge their own and other kids’ strengths and differences. Provide them with opportunities to display their talents and skills and allow them to notice other kids’ talents as well. Encourage an environment where kids praise one another for their strengths and for their positive performances.

Friendships and social interactions are very important for the development of a good self-esteem. Do you know any other helpful tips on how to develop social skills in a child? Please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
