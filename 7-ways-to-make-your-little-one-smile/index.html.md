# 7 Ways to Make Your Little One Smile Every Single Day

Okay, I am going to be honest with you: so many of my best blog topics are inspired by my daughter. Whenever I sit down to brainstorm, she immediately starts sharing her favorite ideas. She especially loves coming up with fun animal topics and fashion ideas! In fact, she helped me brainstorm this guide on the top 7 ways to make someone smile, originally suggesting her own title, "Top ways to make your child smile." Here are seven simple, joyful ways to bring out that bright, happy expression on your child's face.

## 7. Read to Them

Children genuinely enjoy being read to, and sharing a good book is one of the quickest ways to light up their face. Every time I sit down to read with my little girl, she gets the biggest smile. Finding the right picture book—or a comforting bedtime favorite like a classic one smile book—can turn quiet reading time into pure magic. I love asking her fun questions at the end of each story (and sometimes on every page) to see how closely she is listening. She constantly amazes me by remembering every single detail. If you want to make reading time even more engaging, try these quick tips:

- Pick picture books with colorful, vibrant illustrations
- Use expressive character voices to bring the story to life
- Ask open-ended questions about what might happen next

Just like when you [bake delicious treats together](#3), taking time out for a book builds a lasting bond.

For more inspiration on early reading habits, resources like [Reading Rockets](https://www.readingrockets.org/) offer wonderful guidance for parents.

## 6. Take Them to the Zoo

Oh yes, taking your child to the zoo is practically guaranteed to put a huge smile on their face! That excitement starts during the car ride there, continues throughout the entire park, and lasts all the way home. Even days later, bringing up memories of the animals will bring that big grin right back. I have loved taking my daughter to the zoo for her first birthday, her second birthday, and every year since. By now, she probably feels like she lives there! Visiting an accredited park certified by the [Association of Zoos & Aquariums](https://www.aza.org/) gives children an unforgettable, hands-on connection with wildlife and nature.

## 5. Dance Together

You would be amazed at how naturally kids move to music. My six-year-old has dance moves that put most grownups to shame—and I don't just think so, I know she does! Putting on some upbeat music in the living room and having a impromptu dance party is an instant mood booster for the whole family. It is great exercise, lets everyone release extra energy, and fills the house with laughter. Pairing a spontaneous dance session with a warm [hug and sweet words of love](#1) creates an unbeatable combination for bringing out genuine smiles.

## 4. Write a Story Together

Children have incredible imaginations, and collaborating on a story together is a fantastic way to let their creativity shine. Start with a fun character or wild setting, and ask your child what should happen next. You will be amazed at the hilarious twists and imaginative details they come up with. Writing down their ideas and illustrating them together gives kids a wonderful sense of pride in their own storytelling ability.

## 3. Baking

My daughter absolutely loves baking cakes and treats with me in the kitchen. We skip the toy ovens and work with the real thing! Naturally, safety comes first—she knows to keep a safe distance whenever the oven is open because it is hot, hot, hot. But she thoroughly enjoys measuring ingredients, pouring flour, and mixing up cake batter or muffin mix. If you are looking for easy kitchen projects to try with kids, here are a few crowd-pleasers:

- Homemade chocolate chip or oatmeal cookies
- Layered fruit and yogurt parfaits
- Simple banana or blueberry muffins

Much like an exciting trip to see the [animals at the zoo](#6), spending hands-on time in the kitchen creates delicious memories and big smiles.

## 2. The Family Pet

Bring out the family pet and watch your child's face instantly light up! Pets have a unique way of providing comfort and joy. Recently, when my daughter was feeling under the weather, I told her our dog was coming over to give her a gentle hug. He cuddled right up to her, and she broke into the sweetest giggle—even while complaining that her nose was plugged up. Educational organizations like the [American Kennel Club](https://www.akc.org/) highlight how positive interactions with family pets foster empathy, gentle care, and emotional well-being in growing children.

## 1. Give a Hug and Tell Them You Love Them

I simply had to put this at the top of my list—I couldn't help myself! Every single time I give my daughter a tight hug and tell her how much I love her, she lights up with a radiant smile and echoes the words right back. Children thrive when they feel cherished, safe, and deeply cared for. This is one of those simple, powerful daily gestures that should never be left unsaid.

There you have 7 simple, heart-warming ways to make your little one smile! Which of these activities does your child love the most, or what special trick always brings out their smile? Share your thoughts and favorite family moments with us!
