# 7 Best Diaper Brands to Use ...

Diapers are an essential need for babies, so here are some of the best diaper brands to use! Did you know there are a two types of diapers you can use? There are disposable diapers and cloth diapers. I personally used disposable diapers for both of my two kids, but I have done my research on cloth diapers, too! There are many pros and cons to both types, and here is my list of the best diaper brands!

## 1. Huggies

I have used Huggies for both my kids, and believe it is one of the best diaper brands! Not only does the company give back for every purchase of diapers but these diapers always do their duty! With every purchase of Huggies diapers, Huggies gives a portion to a "Every little Bottom" and to "Feeding America." Every Little Bottom provides a clean diaper for a baby in need for a day! Because imagine having to reuse soiled diapers? 1 in 20 American moms struggling with diaper needs and have cleaned out and reused wet or soiled diapers. That's no bueno!

## 2. Bumgenius

Bumgenius is a cloth diaper made from organic elements! Now, it's really a preferential thing when it comes to choosing cloth vs. disposable diapers. Although, cloth diapers are a little pricey to start out, you save more than $1,200 over buying disposable diapers! Cloth diapers are a little tricky, since you do have to clean them yourself and they do take awhile to get accustomed to... but there are a lot of pros for your baby!

## 3. Pampers

Pampers is another great disposable diaper brand that I have used! They're soft and flexible, and depending on your baby they can hold a good amount of liquid... and solids. *wink Pampers is one of the popular leading brands in disposable diapers! They also have a great rewards program that you can join for free and redeem great prizes! Go Pampers!

## 4. FuzziBunz

The FuzziBunz One-Size Diapers are the most adjustable and innovative one-size cloth diapers on the market today! Never before has a pocket-style cloth diaper been SO adjustable! These diapers fit 7 - 35lb babies, and can adjust to fit your munchkin! Along with other cloth diapers, this brand also helps in durability and longevity! Plus, they have a lot of cute designs and colors!

## 5. The Honest Company

The Honest Company was founded by Jessica Alba and Christopher Gavigan, who created this company for their kids! They create safe, chemical-free, organic, toxic-free, natural diapers and products! You can also go to [honest.com](http://www.honest.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and get a free trial on products and diapers! They have fun designs for diapers, and their wipes are biodegradable and plant based! I got myself a free trial, and now I am a member of the company and I purchase their products from cleaning supplies to diapers and wipes! Check them out!

## 6. GDiapers

gDiapers is another brand of cloth diapers, and this brand is biodegradable you can flush the diaper inserts, use them as compost, or you can simply toss them in the trash! This brand also has great designs, and colors, and they specifically use Velcro straps as opposed to snap buttons. But, like every cloth diaper, the choice is yours and what works best for your baby! I've had friends who have used gDiapers and absolutely loved it, and I've also had friends who used it and switched! You be the judge!

## 7. Seventh Generation

Seventh Generation also creates natural household products! Their diapers and wipes contain absolutely no fragrances, latex, petroleum-based lotions or chlorine processing. Plus, from Dr. Seuss, this brand is Lorax approved! This is because Seventh Generation helps protect nature and our environment! Who doesn't love using good products like this that can help our Earth? I sure do!

There are numerous products out there that can benefit our babies, but the choice is really up to you and what you feel best suits your baby's bottom! These are some brands that I have just loved and learned about as well! I'm sure there are others out there that I've missed! What are your favorite brands?
