# 37 Inspiring Lunchbox Ideas for Kids This School Year ...

If your kids are anything like mine, it can be hard to please them day after day, so lunchbox ideas are something you're always on the hunt for. You want your kids to eat their lunch, but you also want it to be something healthy and satisfying so it will carry them through the afternoon in the classroom. If you're at your wits end, check out these lunchbox ideas and you'll be well on your way to being the mom who packs the best lunches.

## 1. Leftovers

![Leftovers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/af/dz/lyer0jf8_338x450.jpg)

Via [Adult Paleo Lunch Box Sausage ...](http://thatsavagelife.com/2013/12/04/adult-paleo-lunchbox-8/)

If you have some nibbles left over from the night before, they are perfect for a flavorful lunch. Leftovers are one of my go-to lunchbox ideas!

## 2. Just the Favorites

![Just the Favorites](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/yo/facn3da8_600x450.jpg)

## 3. Lots of Fruit

![Lots of Fruit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/ji/zgfi06o0_500x375.jpg)

Via [10 Easy, Healthy Lunchbox Ideas](https://www.redbookmag.com/life/mom-kids/advice/g483/healthy-lunchbox-ideas/)
