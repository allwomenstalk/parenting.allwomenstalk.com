# 47 AWESOME Kids Crafts Your Little One Will Love ...

If you have little ones at home, it really pays to have some kids crafts you can do with them. Not only do arts and crafts projects promote several areas of your child's development, but they also give you both something fun to do together when days at home get boring. Check out these really fun kids crafts and you'll have so much to do you won't know where to start.

![Soup Can Bird Feeders](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/nw/hbtg7ybn_567x768.jpg)

Via [Kids](https://www.hugedomains.com/domain_profile.cfm?d=plumadorable.com)
