2 Toilet Paper Roll Owls

All you need to create this cute little guys is a slightly flattened toilet paper tube, some craft paper and eyes and you're good to go.

This craft is a great way to keep your little ones entertained while also teaching them about creativity and resourcefulness. To make these adorable Toilet Paper Roll Owls, all you need is a slightly flattened toilet paper tube, some craft paper, and eyes. It's a simple, yet fun project for kids of all ages. 30 Kokeshi Dolls

Via Yahoo - login
I love this craft for using up leftover scrapbook paper. Look for templates online or draw your own.

***

Creating Kokeshi Dolls is not only a fantastic way to recycle remnants of paper, but it's also a delightful way to introduce kids to Japanese culture. These dolls, with their simple lines and minimalist aesthetic, conjure a charming appeal that entices the little ones to personalize with their own color choices and designs. Children can pick their favorite patterns to create a small family of dolls, or even make a set as a gift. The best part is watching your child's face light up with pride as they showcase their very own handmade collection. Extra tip: Use markers or paint for facial features to bring the dolls to life!
