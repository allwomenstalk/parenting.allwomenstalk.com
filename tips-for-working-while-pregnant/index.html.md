# 7 Tips for Working While Pregnant ...

Working while pregnant is not without challenges. You can certainly go forward with your career when you are pregnant but these tips can help make that easier for you. There are certain steps you can take to make sure that your pregnancy does not cause any issues with your job. These are things you may not have considered that can help you when you are working while pregnant.

## 1. Start Thinking about Childcare Now

One of the important things to do when you are working while pregnant is begin thinking about childcare. Childcare is something that is of great importance. It is not always easy to find quality childcare. That is why it is important for you to begin your search now, so that lack of childcare will not be an issue later. By searching now, you won’t be rushed in such an important decision.

## 2. Tell Your Boss before the Grapevine Does

Telling your boss you are pregnant can be difficult. You may fear that they will not react well. It is still a better decision to tell them before they hear it through the grapevine. In fact, they should really be the first person you tell at your workplace. Most bosses will appreciate you being upfront and honest with them.

## 3. Still Bring Your a Game

If you want to continue to advance in your career, you have to continue making your best effort, even when you are pregnant. This does not mean that you don’t put your pregnancy first. Of course you need to do whatever you need to do for the health and safety of yourself and your baby. It simply means you continue to give your best effort at work. It is always clear if you are trying your best.

## 4. Be Clear about Your Pregnancy Needs

Your body goes through a lot of changes when you are pregnant. You may need more bathroom breaks or more breaks to stretch your legs later in your pregnancy. The health of yourself and your baby should be your first priority. It is best to be clear about what your needs are for your pregnancy. Letting your boss know in a clear manner helps them to be more understanding.

## 5. Don’t Use Your Pregnancy as an Excuse

Using your pregnancy as an excuse is very different than taking care of yourself and your needs when you are pregnant. Using your pregnancy as an excuse is not a smart career move. Dishonesty almost always shows through to others. Others can see the difference between being careful for the sake of your pregnancy and using it as an excuse. They will respect you if you still display good work ethic.

## 6. Stock Your Desk with Pregnancy Essentials

You spend a lot of time at your workplace. It makes sense to stock your desk or locker with some pregnancy essentials. It can make your time at work much more pleasant for you. Keep some snacks and bottled water on hand. You also want to keep things like Preggie Pops, Tums and any other item you need to make your days easier.

## 7. Let Your Co-workers Share in the Fun

Everyone loves to enjoy the pregnancy of others. So share your pregnancy experiences with them. The key here is to make sure you keep things balanced. You want to share but in moderation. Don’t allow it to be the only thing you ever talk about.

Working while pregnant is a unique situation. What tips have you followed to make working while pregnant a good experience? Please share your tips!
