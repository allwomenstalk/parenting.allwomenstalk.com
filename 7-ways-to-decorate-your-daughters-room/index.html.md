# 7 Ways to Decorate Your Daughter's Room ...

Decorating your daughter’s room can be a lot of fun. Girls’ rooms tend to inspire romance and sweetness in comparison to boys’ rooms. You can create a wonderland for your precious daughter by ensuring that all the right elements are present in her room. Here are some fantastic ways to decorate your daughter’s room.

## 1. Canopy over the Bed

Image source: [s3prod.weheartit.netdna-cdn.com](https://goo.gl/jjHZX2)

While your daughter’s room may not be big enough to hold a canopy bed, you can still create a canopy over her bed. Get a hook over a bed and fix a hanging ring to it. Then use sheer white or pink fabric to create a canopy that puddles over the floor for the princess’s bed.

## 2. Make a Headboard

Create a headboard for your daughter’s bed with pastel color fabrics. Get square wooden boards, and place a layer of foam on them. Then tightly wrap the fabrics over them and finish by tying a bow with satin ribbons. Attach these boards side by side on the wall to create a headboard.

## 3. Choose the Right Colors

Anyone will tell you that the best colors for a girl’s room are pastels. They are soft, creamy and immensely dreamy. While pink is an all-time favorite, you do not have to restrict yourself to it. There are a wide range of pastels to choose from.

## 4. Choose the Right Linen

Image source: [s3prod.weheartit.netdna-cdn.com](https://goo.gl/E1A8en)

This is one the ways to decorate your daughter’s room where you need to take her personality into account. While the overall color scheme of the room may be pastel, depending on your daughter’s personality, you can choose bed line that either goes with the theme or contrasts it. For instance, you can have a dark orange bed spread in a peach colored room to create a contrast.

## 5. Stars on the Ceiling

This is one of the ways to decorate your daughter’s room that will allow her to dream and aspire. Put stars on the ceiling that are only visible in the dark and let your daughter gaze into them night after night and make her own dreams.

## 6. Choose the Right Lighting

Lighting is very important in a girl’s room. On one hand, it needs to be bright enough so she can apply makeup and admire her clothes and on the other, it has to be dim enough to be romantic. Supply your girl with the right choices at the right places.

## 7. Artwork on the Walls

This is one of the other ways to decorate your daughter’s room where you need to again consider the type of person she is. Ensure that the artwork reflects who she is or who she aspires to be, whether you are using toys, paintings or abstract art.

There are so many ways to decorate your daughter’s room that you can let your imagination take hold. The important thing to remember is that your daughter needs to feel comfortable in the room as well as take pleasure in the time that she spends in it.
