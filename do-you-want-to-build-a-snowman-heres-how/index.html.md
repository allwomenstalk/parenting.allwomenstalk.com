# Do You Want to Build a Snowman? Heres How!

If you have kids, you know that they love to go outside and get crazy in the snow. And if your kids are anything like mine, they want you to come outside and play too. It's not my [favorite thing to do](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/of-my-favorite-things-to-do-in-summer/), but it makes my kids happy. Looking for something to do out in the snow with your kids? How about building a snowman?

## 1. Look for a Patch of Packable Snow

![Look for a Patch of Packable Snow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ni/qn/u63hu360565dd5c28e54e320292031_600x394.jpg)

That light, fluffy snow won't stick together. You need to find some snow that you can pack together.

## 2. Start Making a Large Snowball

![Start Making a Large Snowball](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ni/or/tnzidsym565dd63a42f0b388713129_600x600.jpg)

Use your hands to start creating a snowball. Let your kids help you out.

## 3. Roll It into a Larger Ball

![Roll It into a Larger Ball](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ud/a9/c1dua380565dd5f210650744119900_600x482.jpg)

Now, place the snowball on the ground and roll it to gather more snow.

## 4. Make a Medium Sized Snowball

![Make a Medium Sized Snowball](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ux/bk/ybvf8dex56622705b0c53446408841_600x413.jpg)

Repeat steps #2 and 3# to make another snowball that is a bit smaller than the one you just made.

## 5. Now Make a Small Snowball

![Now Make a Small Snowball](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i8/kc/iavjxjvg5662278be0d66254485184_600x401.jpg)

Do this again to make a small snowball for a head.

## 6. Stack the Snowballs

![Stack the Snowballs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iu/v0/druiw9sr565dd75d498c8297506298_600x400.jpg)

Pile the medium snowball on top of the large one and the small one on top of the medium one, pressing down gently to hold the snowballs in place.

## 7. Pack Some Snow in between the Snowballs

![Pack Some Snow in between the Snowballs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gx/un/e6fd69kv565dd7f061c74944807352_597x400.jpg)

Show your kids how to pack some additional snow between the pieces of the snowman's body. This will help hold the entire thing together.

## 8. Add Body Parts

![Add Body Parts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fn/jx/nnmk2rs4565dd7a1ce640744563520_600x450.jpg)

Gather some sticks from around your yard and insert them into the sides of the snowman to make arms.

## 9. Create a Face

![Create a Face](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/qx/v0o8hud4565dd6b359da2826938385_533x400.jpg)

Let your kids use rocks and a carrot to make a face on your snowman.

## 10. Accessorize

![Accessorize](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/jb/uuj2psmw565dd9197307b823194897_573x400.jpg)

Now, add a hat, gloves or a scarf to bring your snowman to life.

## 11. Keep It up

![Keep It up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/bw/pswlolk9565dd6e9d3f30721430672_545x400.jpg)

If you want to make your snowman last for a few days, have your kids check on it and make adjustments to keep him standing.

## 12. Take Pictures

![Take Pictures](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e6/un/pr8nvkre565dd98220dda006104847_600x450.jpg)

Don't forget to snap some selfies with your snowman so you can remember him forever.

Are you going to build a snowman with your kids this winter? What other tips can you add?
