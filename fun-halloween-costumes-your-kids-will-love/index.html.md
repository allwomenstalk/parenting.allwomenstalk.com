# 7 Fun Halloween Costumes Your Kids Will Love ...

As our children grow up they want more freedom to pick out their outfits without any help from their parents and this includes **Halloween costumes for kids** as well! They don't want mom or dad picking out the adorable pumpkin or princess costume, they want to be something fun and something that will impress their friends. Here are **7 Fun Halloween Costumes Your Kids Will Love**...

## 1. Super Mario Bros: Gamer Halloween Costumes for Kids...

![Super Mario Bros: Gamer Halloween Costumes for Kids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/parenting/2011/10/mario_428x428.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

My friend's boys dressed up as **Mario** and **Luigi** last year and I couldn't get over how cool they looked! **The Super Mario Brothers** have been around since I was younger and now our kids are able to experience the same fun with [Nintendo](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/active-wii-games-for-kids/)that we had. This is a great **Halloween costume for kids** that everyone will love and be impressed by, maybe you could even get your dog to dress up as [Yoshi](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)!

## 2. Ride Em' Cowgirl: Country Halloween Costumes for Kids...

![Ride Em' Cowgirl: Country Halloween Costumes for Kids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/parenting/2011/10/cowgirl_428x428.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Young girls love [horses](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/things-to-do-while-young-the-ultimate-bucket-list/)and dream about riding them some day. With this fun cowgirl **Halloween costume for kids**, little girls won't have to wait for their country dreams to come true! Grab a cute pair of boots, teach her how to talk with a southern accent and your little **cowgirl** is all set to trick o' treat.

## 3. Ahoy Matey: Pirate Halloween Costumes for Kids...

![Ahoy Matey: Pirate Halloween Costumes for Kids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/parenting/2011/10/pirate_430x430.jpg)

Photo Credit: [ToysRUs](https://www.toysrus.com/)

Argh! This little [pirate](https://allwomenstalk.com/happy-international-talk-like-a-pirate-day-ye-scallywags/)is cool with his skull cap and sword and he will be so excited to show off his **Halloween costume** to all his friends. Mom and dad can be happy with **Halloween costumes for kids** that are something they think is cool yet we still think is cute and adorable! If you give your children a few options to choose from they will feel like they have the freedom to pick, yet you will be pleased with their choice.

## 4. Cast a Spell: Witch Halloween Costumes for Kids...

![Cast a Spell: Witch Halloween Costumes for Kids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/parenting/2011/10/witch_428x428.jpg)

Photo Credit: [ToysRUs](https://www.toysrus.com/)

She's a good little **witch** who will have fun casting spells in her spider dress, hat, and striped tights. I don't know about you, but I think [witches](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-awesome-monsters-to-be-this-halloween/)are one of the most popular **Halloween costumes for kids** no matter what movie, cartoon, or princess costume is out that year. All she needs is a big witch hat, a broom to fly on and your little girl is ready to knock on some doors for her candy!

## 5. Harry Potter: Wizard Halloween Costumes for Kids...

![Harry Potter: Wizard Halloween Costumes for Kids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/parenting/2011/10/harrypottercostume_428x428.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Dress your little girls up as witches and your little boys as **wizards** with this fun Harry Potter **Halloween costume for kids**! The robe and accessories come separate, but you can buy them all for about $30 or less. Children everywhere were crazy over the [Harry Potter](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-reasons-people-love-harry-potter-books/)books and movies and now that the spells are over your kids will be able to relive the adventure over and over in a great costume like this.

## 6. Smurfs Character: Movie Halloween Costumes for Kids...

![Smurfs Character: Movie Halloween Costumes for Kids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/parenting/2011/10/smurf_432x432.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Smurfs are another great [cartoon character](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-cartoon-kids-i-love/)that I was happy to see come back for new generations to enjoy. Whether kids want to be a smurf or smurfette, this will be a fun **Halloween costume for kids** that parents will be excited about too. You can even buy [blue hands](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)to go with the costume!

## 7. Police Officer: Hometown Hero Halloween Costumes for Kids...

![Police Officer: Hometown Hero Halloween Costumes for Kids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/parenting/2011/10/cop_428x428.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Kids look up to **police officers** and **firefighters** and hope to some day be a [hero](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-ways-to-welcome-your-hero-home-from-overseas/)just like them. We might think this little guy looks sweet and adorable, but let him know how cool and tough he looks when he wears this cop** Halloween costume for kids** that will make all the little girls smile! Your little girl will also have fun in this costume and look just as cool as her brother did the year before.

There are so many **Halloween costumes for kids** to choose from and it can be exciting but also exhausting if you have a picky child. Since you don't want to be at the store all night long waiting for your little ones to make their decision, pick out your top four that you are comfortable and happy with and let them have the "freedom" of choosing the one they really want. They are still very young and might not make the best choice without mom or dad's help, but we want to let them to know they are growing up and **halloween costumes for** **kids** can be that little step for them towards feeling like a "big kid".
