# 7 Fun Ways to Make Memories with Your Kids This Fall ...

There so many fun ways to make memories with your kids this fall! Sure, there's the cider mill and pumpkin patch, but let's get creative! Your kids are only young once, so let's make some memories. These are some of the best ways to make memories with your kids this fall.

## 1. Have Family Pictures Done

One of the best ways to make memories with your kids is to have your family pictures done outdoors. You can take advantage of all nature has to offer as a backdrop. You can choose outfits that will go beautifully with the changing leaves. This is something you'll be able to look back on and enjoy for years to come. It's the perfect way to capture a fall memory that you won’t soon forget.

## 2. Have a Pumpkin Carving Party

Pumpkin carving parties are always fun! You can keep it small and centered around your family or go a little bigger and invite your entire block. If you have concerns about your children being around all the sharp objects necessary for carving, you can have a pumpkin decorating party instead. Use glitter, paint, yarn and whatever other decorative accents you can gather up. For a special treat, toast the cleaned pumpkin seeds in the oven until they are nicely browned then add butter and salt. Yum!

## 3. Go Apple Picking

Fall is the ideal time to go apple picking. If you're lucky enough to live near an apple orchard, take advantage of it! Go apple picking and then use the apples to make some delicious recipes. Apple sauce and apple deserts are always a hit with kids. You can even freeze sliced apples to enjoy throughout the winter ahead.

## 4. Have a Weenie Roast

Weenie roast, hot dog roast, bonfire: they are all pretty much the same thing. Grab some sticks and toast your hot dogs and enjoy with all your favorite trimmings. Your kids will love to roast marshmallows or even make s’mores. A weenie roast is an alternative supper that your kids are sure to love. It also gives mom the night off from cooking!

## 5. Play in the Leaves

Leaves are something that adults see as a chore. Children see them as an opportunity for fun. And while you may need to do some yard cleanup, why not let your kids have fun first? It's something they'll love doing and have fond memories of. You could even enjoy jumping into the leaves with them. Why not?

## 6. Go Hiking

Hiking is a great way to make memories with your kids in the fall. The unbearable heat of summer has passed and you can enjoy the nice weather outdoors. Fall is a beautiful time of year for a hike, as well. And sometimes you get lucky and have some moments of bonding with your kids when you are out in nature. They may even open up to you and share bits of their world while you walk.

## 7. Make Homemade Hot Cocoa

How could anyone not love homemade hot cocoa? Parent or child, this one is a hit for all. You can make it extra special by having marshmallows, whipped cream, sprinkles and chocolate syrup for drizzling on hand. This is a great activity for days when the weather may not permit for outdoor options. It is also a perfect way to start a lazy Saturday morning.

These are some ideas of things you can do to make memories with your kids this fall. What are your fall plans with your children? You could inspire someone else by sharing your ideas!
