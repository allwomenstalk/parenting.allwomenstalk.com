# 33 Adorable Teddy Bears for Your Child to Love ...

Every little kid loves teddy bears, so they make an all around perfect gift for your little ones. Or the kids of your friends if you don't have your own munchkins yet. Teddy bears are great for playing with, but also make excellent decorations for a bedroom. The children in your life will love having teddy bears as friends. Check out these super cute examples and you'll see just what I mean.
